## Requirements
1. Enclosure must promote airflow with top and bottom intake vents and side exhaust vents at heatsink height that follow active cooler airflow.
2. All ports must be accessible without opening the case. 4 USB, ethernet, USB-C, 2 micro HDMI, and SD card slot.
3. Onboard LED must be visible through small cutout.
4. Pi must click into base and release with minimal effort when case is open.
5. Case must open and close easily with a secure snapping or latch mechanism that stays closed in any given orientation.
